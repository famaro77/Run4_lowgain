# Run4_lowgain
Repository with analysis and study of the various Run4 lowgain data sets

## Main goal
Different combination of VGEM and drift field in low gain were taken during Run4. Analyzing the <sup>55</sup>Fe daily scans, the amount of saturation can be estimated (as well as diffusion). Normalizing background data to calibration datasets and looking at the energy spectra, the energy threshold and effect on he spectrum can be checked with the various types of datasets.
